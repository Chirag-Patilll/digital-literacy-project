# 🛡️ Social Media Etiquette Guide for College Students

> **Mission:** As a Student Digital Ambassador, I have compiled this checklist to help my peers navigate the digital world safely and responsibly.

---

| ✅ The Do's | ❌ The Don'ts |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Privacy First:** Use strict privacy settings to control who can see your personal posts and data. | **Oversharing:** Don't post sensitive personal information like your home address, phone number, or OTPs. |
| **Fact-Check:** Verify the source of any news or academic info before sharing it to prevent misinformation. | **Cyber-Conduct:** Don't engage in cyberbullying, use offensive language, or post aggressive comments. |
| **Stay Professional:** Keep profiles like LinkedIn and GitHub updated with your academic credentials. | **Privacy Rights:** Don't share photos or videos of others without their explicit permission. |
| **Profile Clarity:** Use a professional and clear profile picture for networking and internship accounts. | **Real-Time Safety:** Don't overshare your real-time campus location or daily schedule for security reasons. |
| **The "Recruiter Test":** Ask: "Would I be comfortable with a future professor or recruiter seeing this?" | **Security Risks:** Don't click on suspicious links, "free giveaway" ads, or unverified scholarship offers. |
