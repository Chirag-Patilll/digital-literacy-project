# Case Study: UPI/Online Payment Fraud
**Type:** Social Engineering & Payment Fraud

## Description
This crime involves tricking users into sending money under the guise of receiving a refund or prize. The core of the scam relies on the victim's misunderstanding of how UPI PINs work.

## How it Happens
1. **Contact:** Scammer calls pretending to be a bank or courier official.
2. **Instruction:** They claim the victim needs to "verify" their account to receive a payment.
3. **The Trap:** A "Collect Request" is sent to the victim's UPI app.
4. **Execution:** The victim enters their PIN, thinking they are "receiving" money, but they are actually "sending" it.

## Target Audience
Frequent online shoppers and individuals who are new to digital payment ecosystems.

## Consequences
- Instant loss of funds.
- Compromised account security.
- Loss of trust in digital financial systems.
