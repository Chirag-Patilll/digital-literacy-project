# 🛡️ Stay Safe Online: Checklist for Students

As a Digital Ambassador, I made this list to help my peers stay safe. Follow these simple steps to protect your accounts!

### ✅ The "Must-Do" List
- [ ] **UPI PIN Rule:** Remember, a PIN is only needed to **SEND** money. You never need a PIN to receive money.
- [ ] **Check the Link:** Before clicking, look at the URL. If it says `amozon.in` instead of `amazon.in`, it’s a fake!
- [ ] **Lock Your Profile:** Keep your Instagram and Facebook profiles private so strangers can't see your personal info.
- [ ] **Strong Passwords:** Don't use your name or "123456". Use a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols.
- [ ] **Two-Factor (2FA):** Turn on "Two-Step Verification" on WhatsApp and Gmail. It's like having a second lock on your door.
- [ ] **Public Wi-Fi:** Avoid checking your bank balance or UPI while using free college or cafe Wi-Fi. Use your mobile data instead.
- [ ] **Verify "Help":** If "Customer Care" calls you first, hang up. Only call numbers found on the official website.
- [ ] **Say No to "Quick Money":** If a message says "Earn 5000/- per day by liking YouTube videos," it is a scam. Block them immediately.

### 🚨 How to Report
If you lose money or see a crime, don't be afraid! Report it here:
* **Call:** 1930 (National Helpline)
* **Visit:** [cybercrime.gov.in](https://www.cybercrime.gov.in)

**Stay Alert, Stay Safe!**
